Instructions

  1. Backup your iPhone to iTunes. (Here's how)
  2. Click the square icon below or here to download charactr.
  3. In Finder, double click the .zip file.
  4. In Finder, right click on charactr.app to open the app.
  5. Wait ~30 seconds for us to pull your data. Eventually, a browser window should open.
  6. Make the browser full screen.
  7. Scroll through the charts in the browser and join the mailing list.
  8. When you're done, force quit charactr.

NOTE: Charts and the app itself may take a while to load. For now, you must be on a Mac.
